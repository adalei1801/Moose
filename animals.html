Moose Information

According to Wikipedia, "the moose or elk is a member of the New World deer subfamily and is the largest and heaviest extant species in the deer family. Most adult male moose have distinctive broad, palmate antlers; most other members of the deer family have antlers with a dendritic configuration"

Moose are typically non aggressive towards humans when unprovoked. When provoked as moose has several tells to signal attack. One of these is if a moose stops chewing its food and stres at you. Moose attacks can often be deadly as they tend to use their antlers to toss things into the air and procede to stomp the source of disturbance with their hooves and stop for small breaks. The the target is still moving they will keep this up for as long as necessary. A way to avoid this is to move out of sight and if that isn't possible then curl into a ball and protect your head and torso and don't move even if the moose stops.

Moose can be very large, the average adult size for a moose is 4.6 to 6.9 feet at the shoulder.Moose can also weigh between(on average) 640-1300 pounds. Moose are very fast as well, they can reach 35 miles per hour(for reference Usain Bolt's top speed is 27.3 miles per hour). Moose can also swim very well and can reach depths of 20 feet as well as reaching speeds of 6 miles per hour and keep this pace for 2 hours(for reference Michael Phelps top speed is 6 miles per hour). They usually swim to cool off on hot days are eat what they find on sea floors.
